## Language Policy
- Default artifact language: English.
- Use Korean only if explicitly requested for harness documents.
- Chat language and artifact language are independent.

## Timestamp Policy
- All harness documents include CLI-derived `Generated` or `Updated` metadata.
- Preferred command: `TZ=Pacific/Auckland date '+%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S %Z'`.
- Do not invent timestamps.

## AI-Readable Style
- Use stable headings.
- Use IDs for requirements, decisions, risks, tasks, checks, and migrations.
- Use tables for traceability and routing.
- Mark unknowns as `TBD` or `Unknown`.
- Separate facts, assumptions, decisions, risks, and open questions.
- Avoid decorative prose, emojis, and marketing language.
